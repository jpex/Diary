About this site

Sep 28, 2017

Hello, my name is James. This site is a public-facing log of my professional development. The goal of this site is to log my own growth. For now, my target audience is myself.

Format

Entries are meant to be representations of what has been studied. The limitations of both the medium (a text-based website) and time prevent in-depth entries. There are few restrictions on myself on what and how I develop.

Content

Since this log is new, little content is present. Large goals, projects, motifs, are naturally absent. Overtime, this information may present itself here, both for myself, and for others. “Rome wasn't built in a day” the adage goes.

Focus

My current profession is designer. Therefore, my primary area of focus covers some aspect of design. I may also work on auxiliary aspects of design (e.g. social sciences, development, product, management).

2017 Goals

Since this log has started in October 2017, yearly goals are limited.

  • Record 30 consecutive daily entries
  • Complete at least 75 entries by end of year.
  • Complete one deep focus.
  • Find, and develop a secondary focus.

Colophon

Fonts are Oswald and Libre Baskerville. The site is powered by Jekyll, using GitHub Pages as its host. Design and front-end are my own. First created on September 28th, 2017.
